Flappy The Bird

"Flappy The Bird" is my 2D Unity Android game inspired by the classic "Flappy Bird." Its simple, yet challenging gameplay is enhanced with exciting features, including:

  • A shop system for buying skins using in-game coins.
  • Theme customization options for a personalized experience.
  • Collectible coins rewarding players during gameplay.
  • A vibrant and unique design style for an engaging visual experience.
